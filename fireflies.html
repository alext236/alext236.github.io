Cave of the Fireflies

Key tech: Unity, Blender, Maya

Genre: Action RPG

Platform: PC/Mac

About the game

Cave of the Fireflies is a 3D action game inspired by Dark Souls. It aims to combine exciting combat with intricately designed environment (a cave). The abstract story of the game is mainly delivered through item descriptions as well as the clues left in the environment and is up to the audience’s interpretation. My primary responsibilities are the whole level design, enemy and item placements, enemy aggression pattern and all the story pieces.

Key features:

  • Strategic real-time combat system, where you can dash, make combos, charge and cancel your attacks.
  • Intricately designed level, where every item placement has a meaning behind it.
  • An abstract story that is up to player’s interpretation
