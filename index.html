About Me

I graduated with a Master's degree in Finance and is now working as the data analyst and designer for Blidz - a fast growing startup in Helsinki.

Being a member of the founding team of Blidz, I had the rare opportunity to have a close look at almost every aspect of the startup working environment and the launch process. Currently I mainly work with all-things data as well as economy design in the company: developing internal dashboard, producing graphic materials, designing UI/UX and the app ecosystem.

Along with my business studies, I took Game Design and Production as a minor. Making games has been a lifelong passion, and thanks to this program I got to work with many different people and gained interesting insights into the game development process. Nowadays, game design and data usually goes hand-in-hand, so I believe my experience in both fields is a personal strong asset.
